About Repfire

Repfire is a modern, cross-platform workout tracking mobile app built with React Native and Expo. Track your workouts, monitor your progress, and stay motivated on your fitness journey.

Key Features

  • Create Custom Workouts - Design personalized workout routines
  • Track Sets & Reps - Record weight, reps, and sets for each exercise
  • Workout History - View your complete workout history
  • Progress Monitoring - Track your fitness journey over time
  • Offline-First - All data stored locally on your device
  • Cross-Platform - Works on both iOS and Android

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new workout?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter a workout name, and start adding exercises. You can add multiple exercises and track sets, reps, and weights for each.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes! All your workout data is stored locally on your device using secure storage. We don't collect or store your personal workout information on external servers.

Can I use Repfire offline?

Absolutely! Repfire works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection since your data is stored locally.

How do I delete a workout or exercise?

Navigate to the workout or exercise you want to delete and look for the delete option in the menu. You can also swipe left on list items to reveal delete options.

Can I export my workout data?

Currently, data export is not available, but this feature is planned for future updates. Your data remains safely stored on your device.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, try restarting your device. For continued problems, please contact support using the information below.
